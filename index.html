Rui-Jie Yew

I am a CS PhD student at Brown University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Suresh Venkatasubramanian. I am also affiliated with the Center for Technological Responsibility (CNTR) and the Data Science Institute (DSI). My current research focuses on the policy impact of conceptualizations of algorithmic systems.

Previously, I graduated from MIT CSAIL with an S.M. in Technology and Policy advised by Dylan Hadfield-Menell. I received a Joint Bachelors in Computer Science and Mathematics from Scripps College and Harvey Mudd College.
