Rui-Jie Yew

I am a CS PhD candidate at Brown University, where I am advised by Suresh Venkatasubramanian at the Center for Technological Responsibility, Reimagination, and Redesign (CNTR). I conduct research on AI policy, with a focus on regulatory incentives and their technological effects. You can find my CV here.

Before Brown, I earned an S.M. in Technology and Policy at MIT, where I worked with Dylan Hadfield-Menell in the Algorithmic Alignment Group at MIT CSAIL.

I also have experience in privacy engineering at Google, AI risk management at the National Institute of Standards and Technology, research mentorship as part of the exploreCSR program (where one of my students won the best research project award!), and seminar-organizing for the CNTR Seminar on Technology and Society and the MIT AI Policy group.
